Biography

Anran LI (李安然) is a first-year Ph.D. student at the University of Hong Kong, where she received her Master of Public Health (MPH) in 2023. She also completed her bachelor's degree in Economics (with Honors) at University of International Business and Economics in 2022.
Anran previously worked as a Research Assistant at the School of Nursing, the University of Hong Kong, and was employed by the Hong Kong Society of Gerontology. Additionally, She is affiliated as a young scholar with the Center for Market and Economic Research at Peking University's School of Economics.
